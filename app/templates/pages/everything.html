Mission

The Mission of Big Data for Health Equity Research Collaborative is Advancing Big Data Methods in Population Health to Improve Health Equity

Group Description

This collaborative group focuses on the use of Big Data for health equity research. We are faculty, trainees, and students from universities across the U.S committed to advancing theories, methods, and findings related to the use of Big Data. We investigate the impact of the social, cultural, and built environment on health disparities and identify levers for change. One theme of our research has been the measurement of area-level racial attitudes and quantifying its impact on health using both machine learning algorithms and qualitative approaches.]
